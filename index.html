About SS

THE HISTORY OF SIAMESE SOCIETY

Siamese Society was founded in 2018 by
Aliyah du Toit, who is the sole owner of this adoption agency.
"I knew from the moment i adopted Sigh, my own Seal Point Siamese,
that i needed other people to experience the joys and struggles i experience.
Cats are a handful, having siamese cats is like living with sugar-high kids when they're active and
coma patients when they're tired."

From then on, she has personally been breeding thorough bred seal point
Siamese kittens. Rarely, there will be Lilac Point and Blue Point kittens, but she mostly specializes in Seal Points.

Apart from her home-run breeding famiily, she also saves abused or abandoned Siamese Cats. They are
brought back to her home for rehabilitation directly from her and once theyre fully cared for, they are posted on the adoption website to find their new family.
